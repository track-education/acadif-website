Delete Your Acadif Account

If you wish to delete your Acadif account and associated personal data, you can request account deletion by contacting our support team.

Email: support@acadif.com

Please include the following information in your request:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your institution name (if applicable)
  • Your username or account details

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • User profile information
  • Attendance records associated with your account
  • Account authentication data
  • Associated personal data stored in our system

Deletion Timeline

Account deletion requests are processed within 7 days of receiving the request.

Certain information may be retained for legal, security, or compliance purposes as required by law.
