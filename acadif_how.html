Acadif Solutions

  • Smart Teaching Plan Generator
    Acadif automatically generates teaching plans (daily, weekly, semester or annual) based on the teacher’s timetable and syllabus.

    Teachers can maintain their own digital diary with lesson plans, teaching materials, and teaching aids for each class or topic.

    Acadif suggests assignment topics and question paper ideas for each lesson. Teachers can modify or send assignments directly to students.

    Teachers can initiate automated IVR calls to parents to collect feedback. Acadif compiles responses and generates easy-to-read reports.
