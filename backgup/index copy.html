About Acadif

Acadif is a cloud-based digital attendance system designed for Indian schools and colleges. It offers QR code-based attendance, role management, and more.

Features

  • QR Code Based Attendance
  • Role-Based Access
  • Class-wise Timetable Management
  • Real-time Reporting
  • Cloud Sync & Mobile Access

How It Works

  1. Register your institution
  2. Onboard principal, staff & students
  3. Scan QR for attendance
  4. View real-time data and reports
